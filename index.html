The Project

There are so many skills that you don't learn in school. How do you do your taxes? How do I organzie my life? Wait, you have to wash your dishwasher?! Why can't you have one place where you can learn it all? Our users want the confidence and independence to navigate the adult world in a simple and organized way. Adult-Tings is here to help.

The Problem

We have observed that many users lack confidence in their ability to learn fundamental life skills.

The Solution

We believe that providing an online learning platform for newly independent adults is key to help connect users to life skills they may not have had the chance to learn growing up.

After conducting 5 user interviews, our team discovered that young adults feel there is no current solution that provides consise and easy to understand rescources for learning about common tasks for independent living. They want to learn more about finances, cooking, and time management through experts instead of searching through the sea of social media where information/advice is not always verified as true.

Prototype

Feedback/New Tutorial Requests

Ability to anonymously or overtly share desires for changes and request new contnet to be available.

This feedback would include the option to send in a written request or a video/voice memo to give users a variety of ways of expressing their ideas.

Reccomended tutorials based on history

Curated lists available to users after completing a tutorial as well as on their home page.

Goal Setting/Reminders

Our goal setting and reminders feature will build onto our Favorites feature and provide an additional space for users to set learning goals on their personal Adult-Tings calendar and have reminders set to notify them to stay on track for their goals.

In App Chat

In addition to inviting friends to Adult-Tings, users would now be able to interact and share with friends through a private messenger.
